47Billion is a product engineering and experience design company that delivers state-of-the-art solutions. Before getting this fancy name we used to be called AWT for last 2 years. We provide end-to-end solutions starting from user experience, design, architecting, development, testing, deployment and scaling. We are present in California, USA and India - Bengaluru, Indore and Hyderabad. We have strategic partnerships with various budding startups and established firms in the US, Europe and India.

Apart from helping our partners, we are also developing a product that will disrupt the way people engage with online content.

Our Partners
